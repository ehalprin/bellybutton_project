How to Use this Dashboard

Select your unique ID number from the dropdown menu to see your personalized bellybutton bacteria data. Your demographic data will appear in the "Demographic Info" panel. The top 10 bacteria types found in your bellybutton will be ranked in descending order in the bar chart. The gauge will show how frequently you wash your bellybutton. And the bubble plot below will show the comparative sample size of each bacteria found. Please contact the research team if you have any further questions!
